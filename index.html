About HMS

Thanks for using this Web application. The aim of this web application is to ensure that the Health Record of all Citizen is stored Electronically. This makes it easy for Medical Practitioners to have an idea ofprevious Health Challenge of each Individual.
As a new user, you are expected to click on the Sign up button to register. Patients with account should click on the Sign in button to login to their Dashboard.
